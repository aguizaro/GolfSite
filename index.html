In The Rough

About

In The Rough is a multiplayer golf party game that was created as a part of the UCSC Senior Capstone Series for Computer Science: Computer Game Design Majors. This game was built in Unity 3D levaring Unity Services' Netcode for Gameobjects to create a multiplayer experience. The game is currently in development and is being playtested by the public.

Constraints

Our game was designed under the following externally imposed constraints:

  • Accessibility: We designed our game to be playable with a single hand by providing an alternate single hand control scheme. We also designed this game in a way that the player reaches a natural resting point within 2 minutes of gameplay.

Gameplay

Our game is a multiplayer golf party game. The game is designed to be played by 2-5 players. The game is played in a series of rounds. Each round, players take turns hitting their golf ball towards the hole. The player who gets their ball in the hole in the fewest strokes wins the round.
